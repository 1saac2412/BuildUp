About Us

Financial literacy is an undeniable aspect of life. And yet it's a topic under-discussed in education systems, leading to a society of adults who were not given a firm foundation for economic success.

Build Up is a student-led initiative dedicated to making financial education accessible to underserved youth through weekly newsletters, practical tips, and real-world financial guidance.
